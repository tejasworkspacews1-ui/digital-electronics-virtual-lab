Digital Electronics Virtual Lab Overview

This platform is organized to behave like a compact virtual practical room. Each experiment section is designed for hands-on observation, repetition, and self-assessment.

What it teaches

Number systems, Boolean algebra, gates, arithmetic blocks, routing circuits, and flip-flops.

How it works

Pick inputs, observe outputs, compare truth tables, and practice canonical forms and state changes.
